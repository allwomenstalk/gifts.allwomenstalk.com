# Unique Christmas Gift Ideas for Nerdy Girls ...

So every Christmas, I face the same dilemma: what do I get for my fabulously nerdy girlfriend, who has more interests in galaxies far, far away and mythical creatures than in anything you'd find at a regular mall? This year, I was determined not to get her a gift card or a generic piece of jewelry that didn't speak to her one-of-a-kind personality. I wanted something as unique and special as she is, something that would make her eyes light up like the time we first watched "Star Wars" together.

I scoured the depths of the Internet and emerged victorious with **cosplay accessories** and **science-infused trinkets** that she's bound to love. For instance, the **Cosplay Ishtar Earrings** will absolutely thrill her; they'd be a perfect touch to her growing costume collection. And for the days when she's feeling a bit more down to Earth, I found an awesome **Apollo 11 Film Reel Lunch Box** that'll remind her of her fascination with space. For a touch of holiday spirit, the **D20 Icosahedron Ornament** is ideal for our geeked-out Christmas tree. And to add a bit of sophisticated geekiness to her everyday wear, there's the lovely **DNA Teardrop Pendant** that I know will catch her eye. But I can't forget her love for all things Hogwarts, so a cozy **Harry Potter Gryffindor Cardigan** definitely made the list.
