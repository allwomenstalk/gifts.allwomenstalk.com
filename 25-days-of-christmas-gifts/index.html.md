# Festive 25 Days of Christmas Gifts and Crafts ...

Crafting is a family-friendly, budget-conscious, and super fun way to count the 25 days leading up to Christmas. Here we have assembled a splendid collection filled with simple yet memorable DIY crafts and gifts for your inspiration. Let's add a dash of creativity each day to make this festive season extra special! Some of the obvious favorites in this list include: `Peppermint Candy Christmas Ornaments`, `Hand Print Santa Keepsake Ornament`, and `Christmas Tree Waffles`. And wait till you see our `Random Acts of Kindness Elf Arrival Letter` – it’s a great way to instill generosity and kindness into the holiday spirit.
